Hello there!

My name is Ivan Rae. I'm an Artist that focuses on making Graphic Designs and Digital arts. I love what I do and what I create is poured with passion and creativity in mind and with the best effort I can give. Over the past years, I have made designs that were used in big events Award ceremonies and local events.

About Me

I'm also known as "haiiro", an alias that I use as an artist and as a musician. Besides Art, I love music and video games with a passion as well. I have a youtube channel where I upload music covers to which I apply my creativity and my skills as a Graphic Artist. My notable works in Youtube are "Piliin mo ang Pilipinas" Vocaloid Cover and "Rolling Girl" Synthesizer V cover which garnered 847 and 1.8k views respectively. In the future, I'll be adding art and gaming-related content in my channel.

Softwares that I use

I use Adobe Illustrator as my main software for my artwork. Majority of the artworks were purely made in this software. Aside from Illustrator, I can also use Photoshop for editing and creating images and other art softwares like Paint tool SAI. In terms of video-making, I use Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro and Vegas Pro. All the videos that I have uploaded on Youtube were made using these softwares.
